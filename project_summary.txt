VayuGuard Project - Phase 1 Summary

Role: Data Analyst

Completed Work:
1. Created project folder structure.
2. Collected weather data using Open-Meteo API.
3. Collected air quality data using Open-Meteo Air Quality API.
4. Stored raw data in CSV files.
5. Checked data shape, columns, data types, and missing values.
6. Removed rows with missing pollution values.
7. Merged hourly weather data with air quality data.
8. Created final_dataset.csv with 85 rows and 9 columns.
9. Performed EDA to identify highest PM2.5, PM10, and CO values.
10. Performed correlation analysis.
11. Created visualizations for PM2.5 trend, PM10 trend, Temperature vs PM2.5, and Wind Speed vs PM2.5.

Key Insights:
1. PM2.5 and PM10 have a strong positive correlation of 0.918.
2. Wind speed has a negative correlation with PM2.5, meaning higher wind speed may reduce pollution concentration.
3. Temperature has very little direct relation with PM2.5 in the current dataset.
4. Temperature and O3 show a strong positive correlation.
5. Highest PM2.5 was recorded on 2026-06-11 at 16:00.
